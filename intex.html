1.OORUM BLOOD SONG VIWES - 2.0M

Oorum Blood is a trendy Tamil song from the movie “Dude,” starring Pradeep Ranganathan, Mamitha Baiju, R. Sarath Kumar, Hridhu Haroon, Rohini, and Aishwarya Sharma. Sung by Sai Abhyankkar and Deepthi Suresh, with music composed by Sai Abhyankkar and lyrics penned by Paal Dabba, the track is an energetic blend of raw emotion and mass appeal, making it one of the year’s most talked-about releases. With a catchy melody and bold lyrics “Oorum Blood” celebrates themes of confidence, power, and individuality. The title itself stands for a strong bond, symbolizing the unity and spirit of youth. The song visually showcases scenes from Chennai and features lively choreography, reflecting the movie’s fun and friendship theme. The production is handled by Mythri Movie Makers, with the film directed by Keerthiswaran. The lyrics highlight a protagonist who stands out, faces challenges head-on, and inspires others with their bravado. A viral line from the song, “Mannan naa vanthana oor alara,” translates to “When I arrive like a king, the whole town shakes,” emphasizing swagger and self-worth. Throughout the song, lines like “Oorum bloodu, 10000 aura va kondu achchadu” (“the town’s blood boiled, my presence brought a power of ten thousand”) capture the explosive energy and attitude.

2.VIZHI VEEKURA SONG VIWES - 2.5M

Vizhi Veekura is composed, sung, and produced by Sai Abhyankkar starring actress Sannve Megghana, with touching lyrics penned by Adesh Krishna. The track also features serene female vocals by Sai Smriti and additional vocals from Arjun, John, and Vasks. Harmony vocals were arranged and sung by Aditya Ravindran, adding rich layers to the song’s soundscape. Abhyankkar himself played the keys, synth bass, and Spanish accordion, while Viswas Hari contributed on electric mandolin and Robin Sebastian handled both acoustic and electric guitars. The rhythm section was also produced by Abhyankkar, showcasing his multi-talented approach to music-making. Indie music sensation Sai Abhyankkar has released his latest Tamil single “Vizhi Veekura” as part of Amazon Music India’s baelist Listening Party, held in Chennai on July 7, 2025. Unveiled during the Prime Day 2025 campaign, the emotional track is a heartfelt blend of longing, introspection, and nostalgia—hallmarks of Abhyankkar’s signature sound. The song is available exclusively on Amazon Music India’s baelist playlist, a platform dedicated to showcasing rising independent talent. The single builds on Abhyankkar’s growing reputation following the viral success of his earlier hits like “Katchi Sera” and “Aasa Kooda.” With “Vizhi Veekura,” he once again delivers a deeply emotional yet modern track that reflects his ability to bridge genres and generations. “Vizhi Veekura” is a Tamil phrase that can be translated to “when the eyes blink” or “blinking of the eyes” in English.
